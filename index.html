About Holsingerdot
What is Holsingerdot?
Holsingerdot is a development company based out of Charlotte, North Carolina. We provide services ranging from common website touch ups to full software development.

Services

Website Development

Website Touch-up

Website Touch-up is just a "touch-up" of your already launched website. This includes fixing any bugs, problems in code, changing elements within the page, and refreshing servers.

"The finishing touches, or just bringing the life back."

Basic Website

Basic Websites are usually 1-5 pages, and often include basic information regarding services or information. This includes a 1-5 page website design, more complex structures, optional external storage, "design focused" development. A website with a basic purpose.

"A basic website with a basic purpose."

Business Website

While not as complex as a full stack, not as minimal as a basic. This is for the businesses or brands in beginning phases that wouldn't need as much fire power. This includes everything from the basic site with a few differences. You can expect additional stoarge, "functionality focused" design, 10-15 pages, heavy features, and customer-oriented features.

"The just-right basic business website."

Full Stack Development

Full Stack is the most complex of websites. Mostly used for small businesses and startups. This includes customer login, off-site storage, custom servers, full user interaction, and much more. While delivering a professional complexity to the site, the design is nothing short of beautiful. Anything you can think of.

"Absolutely the most bang for your bucks, anything you can think of with no limitation."
